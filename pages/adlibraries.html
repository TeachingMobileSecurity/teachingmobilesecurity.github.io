Ad Libraries

Introduction

An Ad library enables developers to make money from publishing their apps on the Play Store through impressions and clicks.
When an Ad Library is made part of our app it has access to all permissions that the app is granted access to.

This means that if the app is authorized to read a user's text messages, the Ad library will have the same permissions.
This could mean that user's data and privacy is compromised.
