Content Providers

Introduction

Content Providers help an app manage access to data it stores/uses. They provide a way for apps to share data with other apps Content providers are the standard interface that connects data in one process with code running in another process.

The default Contact app has access to all of the user's contact data. However, another app such as an email-client or an instant-messaging app may also need access to contact information.

A security issue may arise if we share sensitive information through a content provider. Another app may get access to and misuse this data.
