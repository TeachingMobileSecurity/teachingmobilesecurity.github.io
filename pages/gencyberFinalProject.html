GenCyber Program at RIT

GenCyber Final Activity

During the camp, you have been exploring numerous types of vulnerabilities. As you've seen, vulnerabilities come in many shapes and forms. For the final activity, you will be examining an existing system for vulnerabilities and report on what you find. You will be evaluated on:

  1. Number of discovered vulnerabilities.
  2. Types of vulnerabilities discovered.
  3. Quality of documentation.
  4. Quality of presentation.
To start, you will be provided with a mobile app that may contain vulnerabilities. You should load this app inside of Android studio and examine it.

Presentation Tips

Creating a good presentation is of paramount importance for properly conveying any message, and can be a huge difference in the success of your project. Some general tips for your presentation are:

  • Make your presentation interesting.
  • Make your presentation engaging. Are you motivated to listen when the presenters speak quietly and aren't interesting?
  • Tell a story.
  • Don't just read off of the slides.
  • Keep it simple and have a core message.
  • Your body tone says alot about your presentation.
  • Rehearse your presentation. Have everyone know their roles.
