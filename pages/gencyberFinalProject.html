Presentation Tips

Creating a good presentation is of paramount importance for properly conveying any message, and can be a huge difference in the success of your project. Some general tips for your presentation are:

  • Make your presentation interesting.
  • Make your presentation engaging. Are you motivated to listen when the presenters speak quietly and aren't interesting?
  • Tell a story.
  • Don't just read off of the slides.
  • Keep it simple and have a core message.
  • Your body tone says alot about your presentation.
  • Rehearse your presentation. Have everyone know their roles.
