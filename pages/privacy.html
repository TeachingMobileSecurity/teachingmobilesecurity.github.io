Data Privacy

Introduction

You store a lot of information on your phone. Think about how this information could be used against you.... People can know your location, name, contacts, text messages, emails and so forth. All of this information can be very harmful to you if others know about it. In this activity, you will learn how easy it is for others to gain access to your sensative information.
