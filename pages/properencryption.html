Proper Use of Encryption

Introduction

All encryption is not created equally. While some forms of encryption may take years to decrypt, others can be easily deciphered with a pen and paper. In this activity, you will:

  1. Learn about some basic forms of encryption
  2. Learn about some basic ways to decipher/defeat encryption
  3. Understand how to implement basic forms of encryption in Android
